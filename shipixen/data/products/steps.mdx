## Product Overview

Steps is your complete fitness companion that turns every step into progress. With seamless Apple Watch integration, advanced workout analytics, and beautiful visualizations, it's the most comprehensive step tracking experience on iOS.

Complete Activity Tracking
- Seamless HealthKit integration for precise step counting and workout data
- Real-time sync between iPhone and Apple Watch
- Comprehensive workout tracking with heart rate, pace, elevation, and GPS routes
- Indoor/outdoor workout detection with environmental data
- Historical data access with smart caching for instant performance

Beautiful Data Visualizations
- Interactive hourly, weekly, and monthly charts with tap-to-explore details
- Calendar view to explore your complete activity history
- Colorful or monochrome chart themes to match your style
- Smart date navigation for easy historical browsing
- Weather-integrated dashboard with location-based conditions

Native Apple Watch Experience
- Dedicated Watch app with real-time step tracking
- Watch complications for at-a-glance step counts
- Background sync keeps your data current across devices
- Manual refresh capability for instant updates

Powerful Home Screen Widgets
- Live step count widgets in multiple sizes
- Interactive hourly step charts on your home screen
- Monthly calendar and trend widgets
- Always current with automatic timeline updates

Smart Goals & Motivation
